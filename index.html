Our Story

Located in the heart of Casablanca, Dar Zaki has been serving authentic Moroccan cuisine since 1985. Our recipes have been passed down through three generations, each dish carrying the warmth and tradition of Moroccan hospitality.

Every meal is prepared with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and spices imported directly from the souks of Marrakech. Whether you're here for a family dinner or a romantic evening, we welcome you to our table.
